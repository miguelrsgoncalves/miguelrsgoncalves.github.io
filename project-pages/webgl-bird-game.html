How to play

Objective

You are a blue bird!

Your eggs are scattered through the forest and you must gather them and put them back into their nest!

Explore the forest and bring them back!

Catching Eggs

When close to an egg, press P to catch it!

Dropping Eggs

When close to the next, press O to drop the egg!

Mission Successful

Gather all five eggs to win!

The Models

The whole project was done in WebGL using code to place every vertex individualy, joining the edges and filling in the faces.

The Animations

The bird animations were done in code by interpolating the translations and rotations of the individuals elements of the bird.

The egg falling animation was done by interpolating its position on the path of a parabola.

The Map

The map is a simple plane where its vertices were translated acording to a height map, giving its height.

The Trees

The trees are 2D images which have a billboard logic to keep them always facing the camera.
