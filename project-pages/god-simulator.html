How to Play

Hand of God

You are god. Your power lies within your hand.

Position you hand in front of the camera and your powers will take effect where you index finger's tip is.

In order to activate your powers, you must touch your index finger and your thumb.

Starting the Game

To start the game you must choose where the civilization will be located.

Choose a nice and cozy place for the miniscule mortal beings and watch as they grow their civilization.

Powers

All powers are related to a specifc hand gesture to activate. Gestures are a combination of extended fingers.

After activation, the Hand of God changes to the color of the chosen power.

Each power has a specifc God Points cost associated, which is shown during gameplay.

Create Farmland

Creates a plot of farmland in an empty grass plot.

Cost

50 GD per plot

Gesture

Index finger extended

Create Forest

Creates a plot of forest in an empty grass plot.

Cost

50 GD per plot

Gesture

Index and middle fingers extended

Scorch

Burns and kills everyone that comes in touch with the power.

Income

500 GD per sould burned

Gesture

Index, middle and ring fingers extended

Final Judgement

Bring the wrath of God to those in range.

Obliterates all beings and plots of land in a large area.

Quite explosive. Use at own risk.

Cost

2500 per Judgment

Gesture

Index, middle, ring and pinky fingers extended

The World

The world is proceduraly generation every time the game starts, making every run unique with differents challenges.

Plots of Land

Grass

Color: Light Green

Free plot of land that can be transformed into a farmland or forest by God.

Water

Color: Light Blue

Plot of land that cannot be transformed into farmland or forest. Workers can traverse water by the use of a boat.

Farmland

Color: Yellow

Plot of land capable of producing food by a farmer.

Forest

Color: Dark Green

Plot of land capable of producing wood by a woodcuter.

No Man's Land

Color: Dark Grey

Plot of land that is left behind the Final Judgement. Has no use nor can it be recovered.

The Civilization

Food

The people consume food during time. The more people there are, the more food they consume.

Houses

Each house can fit 5 people. To build more houses the civilization needs wood. Watch the civilization grow as the people build more houses.

The houses are represented by different shades of brown rectangles that are built around the civilization center.

Workers

In this society, everybody is born with a specific job for life.

Farmer

Color: Red

Goes to a plot of farmland and creates food for the civilization.

Woodcuter

Color: Orange

Goes to a plot of forest and creates wood for the civilization.

Food Carrier

Color: Pink

Goes to the closest food resource produced by farmers and brings it to the civilization to be used.

Wood Carrier

Color: Blue

Goes to the closest wood resource produced by woodcuters and brings it to the civilization to be used.

Tips and Tricks

Population Control

The food consumption is exponentialy increased the more people there are in the civilization. Keep the number of people in check by using Scorch. I know you are their God, but don't get too attached! You are seeing the World from too far away to see their suffering!

God Points Economy

This comes in hand with the Population Control. Always keep your GD in mind, as running out of them can be a free ticket for the civilization to plummet to it's demise!

Time of Travel of Carriers

All workers take time to travel. Carriers are faster than all other workers but they still take time to go around! Keep resource production plots near your civilization center for faster production.

Spread Out the People

Although it's good to have resource plots close to each other, try to spred them out around the civilization in such a way that not all workers clump togheter. If they are tightly packed you may end up taking more lifes than expected when you use Scorch! And you aren't that bad of a God. Right?
