How to play

Simply watch the train go around the track, stopping on the various train stations.

There are some controls to influence the speed of the train and enable or disable some aspects of the 3D world, but the main experience is just sitting back and watching the train.

The Models

The whole project was done in WebGL using code to place every vertex individualy, joining the edges and filling in the faces.

The Animations

The train animations were do by using anchor points in the tracks, then interpolating the train position along the track sections.

The train then stops at the various train stations, accelerating after departure with a smooth movement.
